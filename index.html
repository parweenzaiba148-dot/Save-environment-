What is Environmental Pollution?

Environmental pollution occurs when harmful substances enter the environment, causing damage to nature, humans, and animals. Pollution can affect air, water, soil, and even climate.

Major Causes of Pollution

  • Industrial waste and chemical discharge
  • Vehicle emissions and fuel burning
  • Deforestation and loss of green cover
  • Plastic and non-biodegradable waste
  • Excessive use of pesticides and fertilizers

Effects of Pollution on Earth üåç

  1. Climate change and global warming
  2. Respiratory and health issues for humans
  3. Water and soil contamination
  4. Loss of biodiversity and endangered species

Types of Pollution

Type Causes Harmful Effects
Air Pollution Factories, vehicles, burning waste Respiratory problems, global warming
Water Pollution Sewage, industrial waste, plastic Diseases, death of aquatic life
Soil Pollution Pesticides, chemicals, landfill Reduced fertility, contaminated crops
Noise Pollution Traffic, construction, industrial activity Hearing loss, stress, sleep problems
